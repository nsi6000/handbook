# Management Degree Template

## Undergraduate
### First Year
* Accounting I
* Economics I / Principles of Economics
* Statistics I
* Mathematics I (Single Variable Calculus + Applied Linear Algebra.)
* Introduction to Business Law
* 7 Elective Courses
### Second Year
* Macroeconomics
    - Prerequisites: Economics I.
* Microeconomics
    - Prerequisites: Economics I.
* Accounting II
    - Prerequisites: Accounting I.
* Statistics II
    - Prerequisites: Statistics I.
* Mathematics II
    - Prerequisites: Mathematics I.
* Intermediate Business Law
    - Prerequisites: Introduction to Business Law.
* 6 Elective Courses
### Third Year
* Marketing
* Operational Research
* Public Economics
* Econometrics I
    - Prerequisites: Statistics I, Statistics II.
* Industrial Organization
    - Prerequisites: Economics I, Microeconomics, Mathematics I, Mathematics II.
* Human Resource Management
* Corporate Finance
* Corporate Strategy
* 4 Elective Courses
